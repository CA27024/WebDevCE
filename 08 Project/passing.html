How it works

The Players

Volleyball is played with 2 teams of 6. Each team of 6 is split into multiple different positions which are as follows:

  1. Setter (S)
  2. Oustide Hitter (OH)
  3. Opposite Hitter (OPP)
  4. Middle Blocker (MB)
  5. Libero (L)
as well as support positions such as
  • Defensive Specialist (DS)
  • Serve Specialist (SS)

The Scoring

Each team scores one point at a time and then has to serve if they just won the point. The first team to 25 wins the set except in the 5th set where it goes to 15. Sets are played until a team wins best out of 5.

The Court

The court is 30x60 ft with an 8 foot net (7 foot 4 inches for women) splitting it into two 30x30 squares. Antennas mark the boundaries of the net.
