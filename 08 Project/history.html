A Brief History of Volleyball

1895
William G. Morgan Invents Volleyball in Holyoke, MA
1916
Key developments such as the set and spike originate from the Philippines and quickly become part of the sport
1947
The international govering body for Volleyball, the FIVB, is founded
1949
The first world championship is held for men
1952
The first world championship is held for women
1964
Volleyball makes it's Olympic debut at the Tokyo Games
1996
Beach Volleyball is added as an Olympic sport
